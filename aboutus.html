About Us

company profile

Established in the year of 2005, we “BTI Tex Private Limited” are the leading, Manufacturer, Supplier and Importer of an extensive array of Power Loom, Rapier Loom, Water Jet Loom Machine etc. We direct all our activities to cater the expectations of customers by providing them excellent quality products as per their gratifications. Moreover, we follow moral business policies and crystal pure transparency in all our transactions to keep healthy relations with the customers.

For our accomplishment story, we are grateful to our Mentor Zubair Shaikh, whose continual backing and direction have been useful to us for attaining exponential development in the current market.

Our Infrastructure

Based at Ahmedabad, (Gujarat, India), our infrastructure spreads over a vast area. Equipped with advanced and sophisticated machines, our unit facilitates the fabrication of Textile Machine and Spares in accordance with client's requirement. For maintaining flawless functionality, we timely upgrade the machines installed at our premises. Furthermore, for streamlined operations, our infrastructure is segregated into various departments. Our infrastructure houses following units:

  • Foundry Department (Cupola & Electric Furnace).
  • Melting Department.
  • Casting Department.
  • Sort Blasting Department.
  • Finishing,Fettling & Grinding Department.
  • Erection Of Machine Department.
  • Painting Department.
  • Dismantling Department.
  • Packaging & Forwarding Department.
